Project

Water Pump Simulator Circuit

Project objective

Build an electronic system capable of simulating the control of a water pump with different operation times.

Technical description

An analog circuit with three operating modes was developed to control the filling time of a container: short, medium, and long. The system uses integrated circuits for timing and activates a buzzer at the end of the operation cycle. The system was fully assembled with analog electronics without programming.

Technologies used
  • Analog electronics
  • Timing integrated circuits
  • Operation time control
  • Acoustic indicators
My specific role

Circuit design, component assembly, and functional testing of the system.

Results obtained

A functional system capable of simulating different pump operation times with end-of-cycle signaling was obtained.
