Project

Robot for FIRST 2024

Project objective

Design and build a robot capable of collecting foam rings, launching them toward a specific target, and hanging from a structure, operated by remote control with system status monitoring.

Technical description

A mobile robot was developed with a structural chassis designed to support ring collection and launching mechanisms. The system integrated electric motors, control electronics, and mechanical mechanisms for synchronized launching. The robot was operated by remote control and featured status monitoring during operation. Conceptual design, chassis assembly, component integration, and functional tests were performed until successful participation in the competition was achieved.

Technologies used
  • Electromechanical traction systems
  • Control electronics for actuators
  • Launching mechanisms
  • Remote control system
  • Mechanical and structural integration
My specific role

Participation in the robot design, chassis assembly, integration of motors and electronic components, and testing of the launching and control system.

Results obtained

The robot met the required functions of collection, launching, and suspension, participating successfully in the FIRST 2024 competition.
