Project 5

6 Degree of Freedom Robotic Arm

Project objective

Design and build a robotic arm with six degrees of freedom controlled by Arduino for precise motion manipulation.

Technical description

A 6 DOF robotic arm was developed starting from a conceptual design evaluated among several alternatives. The selected model was designed in CAD, manufactured by 3D printing, and assembled with the necessary electronic components. The system was programmed in Arduino to control each joint of the arm individually.

Technologies used

  • CAD design
  • 3D printing
  • Arduino microcontroller
  • Electronic integration of actuators
  • Motion control programming

My specific role

Analysis and selection of the robotic arm model, mechanical assembly of the arm, electronic integration, and system control programming.

Results obtained

A functional robotic arm was obtained capable of controlling its six degrees of freedom through programming, visible on the Arduino serial monitor.
